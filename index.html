City Lights

Welcome to Montrose: A city built by the players whose characters inhabit it. This is a sandbox, modern realistic supernatural group for players to explore their favorite characters and interact with other players to develop complex relationships and dynamic plotlines. Use your creative freedom to flesh out your favorite heroes and villains, or use the basic universe provided by the mod to help grow one of your favorites.

The Rules

  • Be kind. We have no tolerance for anyone being an unpleasant experience in our room. If you can't show a modicum of respect, and you're not here to be a positive contribution, we have no place for you.
  • Don't annoy the mod. The mod's role is to maintain the page, enforce the rules, and bounce problem players. Don't bother the mod with personal player conflict or backseat moderating.
  • Modern supernatural setting: All manner of character are allowed, so long as they can pass for human.
  • Live-action faces. No artistic renderings, anime, or cartoon depictions. Duplicates and faceclaim hoarding are discouraged.
  • Limit canons. This is not a room to cater to canon characters/storylines, but we will accept them within reason, so long as they fit the setting and aren't ignoring the spirit of the room.
  • Hard limits. We don't care if you want to smut in the room, on the following conditions:
    1. All characters and faceclaims are over the age of 18.
    2. Extremely graphic kinks or violence need to be taken to DMs. Absolutely no gore, vore, bathroom play, or bestiality. These will result in an immediate ban.
    3. You aren't monopolizing the room while others are trying to interact. If you're pushing out other peoples' posts, you need to take it to DMs.
  • Participation: For those wanting to participate, it's best to ask to join a scene unless it's been specified to be open. For those in the scene, be open to letting other people in. If you want to join a scene, make it clear you're joining; putting your character in the same scene but not interacting because you want the other person to make the first move may not give the results you want. Be proactive, and be friendly!
