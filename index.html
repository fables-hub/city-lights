City Lights

Welcome to Montrose: A city built by the players whose characters inhabit it. This is a sandbox group for players to explore their favorite characters and interact with other players to develop complex relationships and dynamic plotlines. Use your creative freedom to flesh out your favorite heroes and villains, or use the basic universe provided by the mod to help grow one of your favorites. Check out our masterlist page for more information on jobs, residents, and rumors!

The Rules

Please read the group rules by clicking here. Failure to read them is not an excuse for breaking them.

The Rules

  • Be kind. We have no tolerance for anyone being an unpleasant experience in our room. If you can't show a modicum of respect, and you're not here to be a positive contribution, we have no place for you.
  • Don't annoy the mod. The mod's role is to maintain the page, enforce the rules, and bounce problem players. Don't bother the mod with personal player conflict or backseat moderating.
  • Modern supernatural setting: All manner of character are allowed, so long as they can pass for human.
  • Live-action faces. No artistic renderings, anime, or cartoon depictions. Duplicates and faceclaim hoarding are discouraged.
  • Limit canons. This is not a room to cater to canon characters/storylines, but we will accept them within reason, so long as they fit the setting and aren't ignoring the spirit of the room.
  • Hard limits. We don't care if you want to smut in the room, on the following conditions:
    1. All characters and faceclaims are over the age of 18.
    2. Extremely graphic kinks or violence need to be taken to DMs. Absolutely no gore, vore, bathroom play, or bestiality. These will result in an immediate ban.
    3. You aren't monopolizing the room while others are trying to interact. If you're pushing out other peoples' posts, you need to take it to DMs.
  • Participation: For those wanting to participate, it's best to ask to join a scene unless it's been specified to be open. For those in the scene, be open to letting other people in. If you want to join a scene, make it clear you're joining; putting your character in the same scene but not interacting because you want the other person to make the first move may not give the results you want. Be proactive, and be friendly!

    Businesses

    Bubble & Brew Co.

    A combination boba/coffee shop in downtown Montrose.

    Silver Spoon Diner

    An old Montrose establishment, this American-style 24-hour diner is dearly beloved.

    The Inferno

    Promoting 9 circles of indulgence, The Inferno is the hottest nightclub in the city.

    Redwood Plaza

    A shopping district in the center of Montrose, it offers an array of stores and cuisine.

    Pixels & Pints

    Any Montrose gamer worth their salt has a story to tell about this bar arcade.

    Reverie Resort & Spa

    A luxurious hotel and spa for those who have some extra cash to spend.

    Sunset Motel

    A by-the-hour motel on the outskirts of town. Use it how you will...

    Gold Star Theater

    A recently revamped movie theater, they show both classics and new films alike.

    Disco Fever Skate Rink

    Another classic Montrose staple, this skating rink still gets packed on the weekends.

    Community Spots

    Meadowlark Park

    A park that includes walking paths, a playground, and a skate park area.

    Montrose Memorial

    One of the leading hospitals in the nation, it's the place to go for any medical needs.

    Montrose Public Library

    After the old library inexplicably burned down, it was replaced with a sleek, modern version.

    The Warehouse

    Once used for factory work, The Warehouse is now used as an event center.

    Harborwalk

    Harborwalk is a public boardwalk bordering the ocean, complete with food, shops, rides, and games.

    Montrose Rec Center

    A recreation center including a gym, pool, entertainment area, and sports courts.

    Residentials

    Northstar Apartments

    Five floors with four apartments per floor. ...The electricity works, mostly.

    Glenndale Street

    One of the older, lived-in neighborhoods, it's a mix of rentals and home-owners.

    The Astoria Residences

    A luxury condominium complex within a gated community; includes a pool and gym.
